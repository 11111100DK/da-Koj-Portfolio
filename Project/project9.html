Project #9

Collectible Card - Vietnam Players

#Card #Photoshop #Illustraytor


Project Overview:

Created an extensive collection of collectible cards featuring Vietnamese football players, covering 7 seasons of the V-League, utilizing Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator.

Scope:

  • Season Coverage: Designed cards representing players from each of the 7 seasons of the V-League, showcasing their performances and achievements.
  • Product Quantity: Produced a total of 2000 individual collectible cards, offering a comprehensive selection of players and designs.

Design Process:

  • Player Selection: Curated a diverse range of players from across V-League seasons, ensuring representation of top performers and rising stars.
  • Visual Conceptualization: Developed unique card designs that capture the spirit of Vietnamese football, integrating player images, club logos, and season-specific elements.
  • Data Integration: Incorporated player statistics, achievements, and biographical information into the card designs, enhancing their collectibility and informational value.

Key Elements:

  • Celebration of Vietnamese Football: Designed cards that celebrate the talent and achievements of Vietnamese football players, fostering pride and recognition within the local football community.
  • Seasonal Variation: Created distinct designs for each V-League season, reflecting the evolution of the league and its players over time.
  • Collectors' Appeal: Crafted cards with high aesthetic appeal and informational value, catering to both avid collectors and casual fans of Vietnamese football.

Outcome:

Delivered a comprehensive collection of collectible cards that serve as cherished memorabilia for fans of Vietnamese football, commemorating the achievements and contributions of V-League players across 7 seasons.
