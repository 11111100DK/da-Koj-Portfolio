Project Overview: da'Koj.art Personal Brand Logo Design

Crafted a distinctive logo for the personal brand "da'Koj.art," specializing in graphic design, using Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator.

Brand Identity:

"da'Koj.art" serves as my personal brand identity, representing your expertise and passion for graphic design.

Logo Concept:

The logo encapsulates the essence of creativity, innovation, and graphic design expertise, embodying your unique style and vision.

Design Process:

  • Conceptualization: Brainstormed concepts to encapsulate the essence of "da'Koj.art" and its dedication to graphic design excellence.
  • Sketching and Drafting: Developed initial sketches and drafts to explore various design directions and refine the chosen logo concept.
  • Digital Design: Utilized Photoshop and Illustrator to meticulously craft the final logo, focusing on aesthetics, balance, and visual impact.
