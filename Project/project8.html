Project #8

Brand Design - SKY labels

#Brand #Photoshop #Illustrator


Business Concept: Collectible Card Sales

Overview: Founded SKY as a brand dedicated to the sale of collectible cards, utilizing designs created from the projects outlined above.

Product Offering: Offer a diverse range of collectible cards featuring designs inspired by football players from around the world, including Vietnamese players and those from various football leagues.

Design Origins: Utilize the meticulously crafted designs from previous projects, such as the World Football Players and Vietnamese Football Players collectible card projects.

Customization and Adaptation: Tailor designs to suit the branding and aesthetic preferences of SKY, ensuring consistency and cohesiveness across all collectible card offerings.

Market Strategy: Implement a targeted marketing strategy to promote SKY as a premium destination for collectors, emphasizing the uniqueness and quality of the card designs.

Expansion Plans: Strategize for future expansion by continually introducing new card designs, collaborating with artists and designers, and exploring partnerships with other brands and organizations in the collectibles industry.

By leveraging the expertise and creativity demonstrated in previous design projects, SKY aims to carve out a niche in the collectibles market, offering enthusiasts and collectors a captivating array of high-quality and sought-after collectible cards.
